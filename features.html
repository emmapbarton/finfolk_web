Features

Two financial lives. One place to see them together.

Most people who are employed and self-employed run two separate apps — one for their job, one for their side income — and do the hard part, combining them, themselves, once a year, under pressure. Finfolk doesn't split you into two accounts. Everything below runs off the same underlying picture, which is exactly why the tax number you get is real, not a guess.

Track

Capture it in seconds, from either side of your income

A quick daily check-in for expenses, mileage, or receipts — tagged personal or business as you go, not reconstructed from memory in January. The moment something's logged, it's already sitting in the right place.
